About Company page


Customer relationship management (CRM) is an approach to managing a company's interaction with current and future customers. The CRM approach tries to analyze data about customer's history with a company, in order to better improve business relationships with customers, specifically focusing on retaining customers, in order to drive sales growth.



[1] One important aspect of the CRM approach is the systems of CRM that compile information from a range of different channels, including a company's website, telephone, email, live chat, marketing materials, social media, and more.
